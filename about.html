Connecting Our Campus Community

Building a trusted marketplace where students can buy, sell, and connect with ease.

Our Story

UniMarket was born out of a simple observation: students had things they no longer needed, and other students needed those exact things. The problem was finding each other. Traditional online marketplaces were too broad, campus bulletin boards were outdated, and social media groups were chaotic.

In 2023, a group of university students decided to build a better solution. We created UniMarket—a platform designed specifically for the unique needs of campus communities. By verifying university emails and creating campus-specific marketplaces, we made it easy for students to connect, negotiate, and transact safely.

Today, UniMarket serves thousands of students across multiple universities, facilitating everything from textbook exchanges to furniture sales. We're proud to be part of making student life more affordable and sustainable, one transaction at a time.
