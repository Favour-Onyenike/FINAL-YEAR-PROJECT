DEPLOYMENT GUIDE: UNIMARKET ON RENDER (FREE TIER)
=================================================

You have chosen the **Free Tier**. To ensure your data is saved permanently (since the Free Tier doesn't support persistent disks), we will use Render's **Free PostgreSQL Database**.

STEP 1: CREATE THE DATABASE
---------------------------
1. Go to your Render Dashboard (https://dashboard.render.com).
2. Click **New +** and select **PostgreSQL**.
3. Settings:
   - **Name**: unimarket-db
   - **Database**: unimarket
   - **User**: unimarket_user
   - **Region**: Oregon (US West) - *Must match your Web Service region*
   - **Instance Type**: Free
4. Click **Create Database**.
5. Wait for it to be created.
6. Look for the **Internal Database URL**. Copy it!
   - It looks like: `postgres://unimarket_user:password@hostname/unimarket`

STEP 2: DEPLOY THE WEB SERVICE
------------------------------
1. Go back to the Dashboard.
2. Click **New +** and select **Web Service**.
3. Connect your GitHub repository.
4. Settings:
   - **Name**: UniMarket
   - **Region**: Oregon (US West) - *Must match your Database region*
   - **Branch**: master
   - **Runtime**: Python 3
   - **Build Command**: `pip install -r requirements.txt`
   - **Start Command**: `uvicorn backend.main:app_with_sio --host 0.0.0.0 --port 10000`
   - **Instance Type**: Free

STEP 3: CONNECT THEM (CRITICAL)
-------------------------------
1. Scroll down to **Environment Variables**.
2. Click **Add Environment Variable**.
3. Enter:
   - **Key**: `DATABASE_URL`
   - **Value**: (Paste the Internal Database URL you copied in Step 1)
4. Click **Create Web Service**.

DONE!
-----
Render will now deploy your app. It will automatically detect the `DATABASE_URL` and use the PostgreSQL database instead of SQLite. Your data is now safe and persistent!
